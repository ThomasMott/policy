Introduction

Welcome to the Keep App ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and disclosed by our app when you use our mobile application ("App").

Our App is designed to help you track and list items you own, without requiring accounts or collecting analytics data. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

Information We Do Not Collect

Our app is designed with privacy in mind. We do not:

  • Collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Require user accounts or login credentials
  • Collect location data
  • Use analytics tools to track user behavior
  • Use advertising identifiers
  • Access your contacts, camera, or microphone (unless explicitly granted permission to add photos of items)
  • Share or sell any information to third parties

Information Stored on Your Device

All data you enter into the app (including item descriptions, tags) is stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to our servers
  • Is not accessible by us or any third parties

Device Permissions

Our app may request the following permissions, which you can choose to grant or deny:

  • Storage access: To save and load your item database on your device

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete your data by:

  • Clearing app data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
